MEET THE BENGAL TIGER - THE KING OF THE JUNGLE...

The Bengal tiger is a big, beautiful cat with orange fur and black stripes, just like a walking painting! 🐯 It has shiny eyes that can see in the dark and a loud “ROAR” that can be heard far away. Tigers love to swim, splash in water, and play hide-and-seek in the forest. They have soft, furry tails and big paws that help them move quietly, like a secret superhero in the jungle.
